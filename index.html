Frequently Asked Questions

Contact us if you have any more questions

What is Qreva?

Qreva is a modern fintech platform designed to make digital payment simple, secure, and rewarding. It allows users to send and receive money, pay bills, and manage their finances effortlessly while empowering agents to earn real-time commissions through seamless transactions and a transparent reward system.

Can I move from personal account to agent account anytime?

Yes, you can easily switch between account types from your settings anytime as long as you complete the Agent account KYC and Compliance..

Difference between personal and agent account?

Personal accounts are for individual users, while agent accounts allow you to process transactions for others and earn commissions.

Must I complete my KYC before using Qreva?

Yes, completing KYC is required to ensure account security and compliance with regulations.

How secure is my money with Qreva?

All transactions are protected with end-to-end encryption and top-level security protocols to keep your funds safe.
